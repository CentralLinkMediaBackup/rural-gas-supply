Est. 1946 — Azle, Texas

A family's promise,
kept for generations.

When Rural Gas Supply Co. delivered its first tank of propane to a farmhouse outside Azle in 1946, it made a simple promise: show up, every time.

Nearly eight decades later, that promise hasn't changed. We're still family-owned. Still based in Azle. Still the people your neighbors call when the tank runs low on a cold January morning.

Three generations of the same family. Tarrant, Parker, and Wise Counties. Thousands of customers who trust us because their parents did.
